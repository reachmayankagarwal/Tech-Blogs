## What is it?

Describe the product in a paragraph or two. What does it do? What problem does it solve?

## How it was built

Walk through the tech stack and key architectural decisions. This is where you can go deep on implementation details — it's as much a dev writeup as a product page.

## Challenges

What was hard? What did you learn? Honest write-ups about the tricky parts are the most useful to other builders.

## What's next

Roadmap, planned features, or what you're working on currently.
